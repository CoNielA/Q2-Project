Philippines: A Leisure Paradise

The Philippines, known as the "Pearl of The Orient Seas," is an archipelagic country composed of 7,641 islands. It is beautfully made by nature with its scenic beaches, fresh green forestry, stunning mountains, colorful cities, and warm-hearted people.

Known for its golden sands and crystal clear water, BORACAY has been a very popular tourist attraction to foreigners and locals.

The perfect cone-shaped volcano, MT. MAYON, found in Albay has been a good place to connect with nature.

Like Boracay, CORON PALAWAN is also known for its crystal clear bodies of water and clean golden sands.
